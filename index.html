Reaction Time Test

Reaction time is a core cognitive metric used in psychology and neuroscience to evaluate how quickly the brain responds to visual stimuli. This free test measures your response speed in milliseconds and gives an instant result.

How it works: click Start Test, wait for the box to turn green, then click as fast as you can. Clicking too early will show Too Soon.

Click Start

Tips to improve reaction time

  • Sleep and fatigue strongly affect response speed.
  • Do 5–10 trials, then use the average, not a single best score.
  • Try again at the same time of day for consistent comparisons.
